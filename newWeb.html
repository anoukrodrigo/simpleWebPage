FIBER OPTICS
Fiber optics revolutionize communication with light pulses traveling through thin, flexible glass or plastic strands. They transmit data faster and over longer distances than traditional copper wires, powering high-speed internet, clear phone calls, and precise medical imaging. As technology advances, fiber optics continue to redefine connectivity and data transmission efficiency.
